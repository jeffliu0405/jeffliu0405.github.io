Risk Management

Core Defense Mechanisms & Risk Engine

When music streams intersect with financial derivatives, manipulation and moral hazards are inevitable. We deployed a 4-tier defense architecture to ensure market stability.

🛡️

1. Anti-Bot Manipulation

Challenge: Click farms orchestrating fake viral Hawkes processes.

Solution: Implementing Asian Options. Fraudsters must continuously purchase streams to maintain the average price over time, exponentially raising the cost of cheating.

⚖️

2. Moral Hazard Prevention

Challenge: Artists buying Put Options and abandoning all marketing efforts.

Solution: Introducing Co-Insurance / Deductibles. Covering only 70% of expected streams, forcing creators to maintain "skin in the game" and active promotion.

🌋

3. Fat-Tail Default Control

Challenge: Music virality creates extreme kurtosis, risking market maker margin depletion.

Solution: Encouraging the issuance of Composite Stream Indices across genres, neutralizing single-asset outlier risks, supported by dynamic margin calls.

📊

4. Model Calibration

Challenge: Traditional BSM assumes continuous prices, failing to map sudden track hype.

Solution: Integrating Prediction Markets with the SVVG Model to capture the Variance Gamma process, properly pricing sudden jumps and skewness.
